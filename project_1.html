Children's Book: from Conception to Print

Year

2021

Work

Graduation Project

Introduction

This children’s book project represents the culmination of my studies and is inspired by my personal story. I carefully designed every element of this book, from the concept and illustrations to the text, paper selection, binding, and printing. This project allowed me to learn how to design a project from start to end, develop a certain discipline in completing a work, and familiarize myself with the world of printing.
